Michoacan River Adventures

Our mission is to help people discover courage, teamwork, and joy through safe and memorable river adventures. Every trip is led by trained guides who respect the river, protect the environment, and make every guest feel welcome.

History

Michoacan River Adventures began in 1998 when a small group of local guides started sharing their favorite river routes with visitors. What began with one raft and a simple love for the outdoors grew into a trusted company known for careful preparation, friendly service, and exciting experiences for families, friends, and first-time adventurers.
